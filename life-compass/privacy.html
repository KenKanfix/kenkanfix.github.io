Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Life Compass respects your privacy. This policy explains how your data is handled.

Data Storage

All data you enter (names, birth dates, locations) is stored exclusively on your device using local storage (localStorage). No data is transmitted, collected, or stored on any server.

No Tracking

This app does not use cookies, analytics services, or any form of user tracking. No personal information is sent to third parties.

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. Lunar calendar data is sourced from the Hong Kong Observatory (public data).

Data Deletion

You can delete any profile at any time from within the app. To remove all data, clear your browser's local storage or uninstall the app.
